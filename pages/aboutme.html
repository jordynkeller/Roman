About Me - Roman

I am a 2 year old pure bred German Shepherd. My birthday is April 15, 2016.
I am my mom's my VERY BEST BOY.
Sometimes I go by Rome, Romie, Bubba, Beef, Buddy, and Ramen Noodle
I am huge, weighing in around 110 lbs (and still growing), but I still think I am a baby.
Dispite my size, I have a very gentle spirit and love hugs and snuggles.
I protect the yard, and my mom. It is my job.
My mom says that I am loyal, energetic, smart (with a little "duhh"), and very handsome!
